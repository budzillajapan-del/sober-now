# SoberNow Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 2026-06-05**

SoberNow is a personal sobriety tracking and recovery support app. This policy explains what data SoberNow handles, how it's stored, and what we do (and don't do) with it. The short version: **everything stays on your device.**

## What SoberNow collects

When you use SoberNow, you may enter:

- Your first name, sobriety start date, and notes about your drinking history
- Sponsor and emergency contact information
- Photos and details about people, hobbies, and events that matter to you
- Daily check-ins (mood, craving level, triggers, HALT status, notes)
- Gratitude entries
- Urge logs
- Slip records and reflections
- Wallet card contents (what you gain by not drinking / what you lose by drinking)
- Twelve Steps progress and personal notes
- Cost-Benefit Analysis entries (SMART Recovery tool)
- Activities you want to do instead of drinking

## Where this data is stored

**All of the above is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.**

- We do not run any servers.
- We do not collect, transmit, sync, upload, or otherwise transfer your data off your device.
- We do not have access to your data. We cannot read it. We cannot recover it for you.
- Your data is not backed up to iCloud unless your device's general iCloud backup is enabled, in which case it is included in Apple's encrypted device backup along with the rest of your apps. We do not have access to that backup.

If you uninstall the app, all your data is deleted with it.

## What we do not collect

We do not collect, transmit, or use:

- Analytics or usage telemetry
- Crash reports
- Advertising identifiers
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Contact lists
- Health or HealthKit data
- Any cloud or account-based information

SoberNow does not have user accounts, sign-in, or any cloud features.

## Permissions SoberNow requests

- **Photo Library**: only if you choose to attach a photo to a reason, person, hobby, or event. Photos are copied (downsampled) into local storage. We do not access photos you don't pick.
- **Camera**: only if you choose to take a new photo from within the app.
- **Notifications**: only if you grant permission for daily check-in reminders and milestone notifications. All scheduling happens on-device.

You can revoke any of these in iOS Settings at any time.

## Tip jar / In-App Purchases

SoberNow is free. If you choose to leave an optional tip through the in-app Tip Jar, the purchase is processed entirely by Apple's payment system. We never see your payment details, billing information, or Apple ID.

Tips do not unlock features. They are gratitude, not a paywall.

## Children

SoberNow is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone.
